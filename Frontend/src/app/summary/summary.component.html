Overview

This scatter plot, with EfficiencyUDP Performance Score on the vertical axis and Computational Cost USD/hour on the horizontal axis, showing that the most efficient models become less expensive to run.

Insights

The graph shows the reduction of computational costs with the introduction of new advancements like LoRA and quantization, which is either increasing or maintaining high efficiency. This means generative AI is becoming increasingly accessible for businesses with low computational capacity and that AI technology is increasingly democratizing.
